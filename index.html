MISERICORDAE PROJECT

A decentralized ‘transformer’ and ‘restorer’ blockchain.

MISERICORDAE has two primary objectives of addressing:

  • Societal Life Issues (SLI)
  • Cryptocurrency Technology Limitations (CTL)

MISERICORDAE Blockchain is a decentralized ‘transformer’ and ‘restorer’ aimed at implementing solutions to peculiar challenges facing cryptocurrency ecosystem. ONNI, is a core-shell hybridized gold-pegged price-superstable and price-hemivolatile cryptocurrency of MISERICORDAE BLOCKCHAIN with hybrid PoW/PoS, nonlinear mining difficulty and multi-algorithmic based.


ONNI was designed to meet the routine needs of ordinary users who desire stability and protection from extraordinary market forces, and also to satisfy the strong interests of currency traders who enjoy price fluctuations as a tradecraft routine.
