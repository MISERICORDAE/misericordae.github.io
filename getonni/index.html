GETTING ONNI

The first instance of Misericordae development is on Ethereum Blockchain, and ONNI can therefore be obtained via Ethereum MainNet using the folllowing information:

  • CONTRACT ADDRESS: 0xBd9c6028e1132A6B52F1ca15C0933A2FD342E21f
  • NAME: MISERICORDAE
  • SYMBOL: ONNI
  • DECIMAL: 18
PRICE: 15 ONNI = 1 ETH. (1 ETH = 1018 wei (type one with eighteen zeros, i.e. 1000000000000000000)
