EXCHANGE ONNI

You can swap or trade your Onni on Exchange Platforms. The following recommended platforms are decentralized, secure, free or very low-cost:


  • KOMODO BARTERDEX PLATFORM - An advanced decentralized exchange that conducts atomic swap between most existing blockchain cryptocurrencies including Ethereum blockchain and its ERC20 tokens. This means that you can enjoy seamless exchange between Onni and Bitcoin, Ether, Litecoin, Dash, Monero,...etc.


  • ETHERDELTA PLATFORM - A decentralized exchange that conducts atomic swap between most existing ERC20 tokens and Ether. This means that you can enjoy seamless exchange between Onni, Ether, and ERC20 tokens. This is a helpful guide to get started on EtherDelta, Guides for First Time Users.


  • IDEX PLATFORM - A decentralized exchange similar to EtherDelta that conducts atomic swap between most existing ERC20 tokens and Ether. You can swap between Onni, Ether, and ERC20 tokens.


  • METAMASK - This is the ideal preference for trading on Ethereum Blockchain. It can be used for peer-to-peer buying and selling through transfers (send). So it is recommended. Go to Metamask Website and choose your browser with the accompanying guides. Chrome Browser is widely used and you can add METAMASK as an extension. It is advisable to store your Onni and Ether in an Offline Wallet like MYETHERWALLET (MEW).
